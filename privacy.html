Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 3, 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy

At ToolBit, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our developer tools at toolbit.dev.

TL;DR: We don't collect, store, or transmit your data. Everything runs in your browser.

Information We DON'T Collect

ToolBit is designed with privacy first:

  • ❌ We don't collect any personal information
  • ❌ We don't store the content you process (JSON, RegEx, Base64, Colors)
  • ❌ We don't track what you do with our tools
  • ❌ We don't require accounts or sign-ups
  • ❌ We don't use tracking cookies
  • ❌ We don't sell or share any data (because we don't have any!)

How Our Tools Work

100% Client-Side Processing:

  • All processing happens in your browser
  • Your data never leaves your device
  • No servers process your content
  • You can use our tools completely offline

Local Storage

Some tools save history to your browser's local storage (localStorage):

  • This data stays on YOUR device only
  • We cannot access this data
  • You can clear it anytime (clear your browser data)
  • Example: JSON Formatter saves your last 10 formats locally

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how people use our site:

  • We collect anonymous usage data (page views, clicks)
  • We DO NOT track what content you process
  • Google Analytics uses cookies (you can block these)
  • This helps us improve the tools

You can opt-out of Google Analytics by using browser extensions like uBlock Origin or Privacy Badger.

Advertising

We may display advertisements through Google AdSense:

  • Google may use cookies for ad personalization
  • You can opt-out of personalized ads at Google Ad Settings
  • We don't control what Google collects
  • See Google's Privacy Policy for details

Third-Party Links

Our site may link to external sites (GitHub, YouTube, etc.):

  • We're not responsible for their privacy practices
  • Please review their privacy policies

Children's Privacy

Our tools are for developers of all ages. We don't knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our tools means you accept the changes.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect your data, there's nothing to:

  • Request access to
  • Request deletion of
  • Request correction of

Your data stays on YOUR device. You control it.

Summary

ToolBit respects your privacy.
Your data = Your device. We can't see it, we don't want to see it, and we don't collect it. All tools run 100% in your browser. We use Google Analytics for anonymous stats and may show ads. That's it.
