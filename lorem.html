üìú What is Lorem Ipsum?

Lorem Ipsum is standard placeholder text used in the graphic, print, and publishing industries for previewing layouts and visual mockups. It's derived from a work by Cicero from 45 BC, "De Finibus Bonorum et Malorum" (The Extremes of Good and Evil).

The text has been used since the 1500s when an unknown printer scrambled it to make a type specimen book. Today, it remains the industry standard because its pseudo-Latin appearance resembles real text without distracting readers with readable content.

Why use placeholder text?

  • Focus on Design: Clients and stakeholders review layout without getting caught up in copy
  • Realistic Proportions: More accurate than "text here" or repeating characters
  • Industry Standard: Designers and developers immediately recognize it as placeholder
  • Variable Length: Easy to generate any amount needed for testing

üñºÔ∏è Why Use Image Placeholders?

When prototyping websites, you often don't have final photography ready. Image placeholders serve several purposes:

  • Reserve Exact Space: Show clients exactly how much area images will occupy
  • Test Responsive Layouts: Verify how your design handles different image sizes
  • Maintain Focus: Keep attention on layout structure, not image content
  • Speed Up Development: Build UI before assets are ready
  • Document Requirements: Specify exact dimensions needed from photographers/designers

The generated images include dimension text by default, making it easy to communicate size requirements to content teams.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Why does Lorem Ipsum look like Latin but isn't readable?

It's scrambled Latin. The original text from Cicero was altered‚Äîwords were added, removed, and randomized. This creates text with realistic letter distribution and word lengths while remaining meaningless.

Is the image generation free?

Yes. We use the public placehold.co service which generates images on the fly. No API key or signup required. Images can be used in development and production.

Can I use Lorem Ipsum in production?

Never. Lorem Ipsum is strictly for mockups and development. Shipping placeholder text to production is unprofessional and confusing for users. Always replace with real content before launch.

Which content style should I use?

Match your audience. Use Classic for formal clients, Corporate for business applications, Tech for developer tools. Fun themes work well for internal demos or creative projects where levity is appropriate.

Why offer different formats (HTML, Markdown, JSON)?

Different workflows need different formats. HTML works for direct pasting into prototypes, Markdown for documentation, JSON for seeding databases or APIs during development.
