üé® The Art of Prompting

Prompt engineering is the practice of designing inputs for AI models to produce optimal outputs. It's like learning to speak the AI's language effectively.

Key principles include:

  • Clarity: Be explicit about what you want.
  • Constraint: Tell the AI what *not* to do.
  • Context: Provide the "who, what, where, why".

üõ†Ô∏è Advanced Techniques

Few-Shot Prompting

Giving the model a few examples of input-output pairs before asking it to do the task. "Here are three examples of good headlines. Now write five more."

Chain-of-Thought

Asking the AI to "think, step-by-step" encourages it to break down complex problems, reducing logic errors.

Role Prompting

Assigning a persona ("Act as a Senior React Developer") sets the tone, vocabulary, and expertise level of the response.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Does the length of the prompt matter?

Yes, but relevance matters more than length. A long, rambling prompt can confuse the AI. A structured, detailed prompt is ideal.

Why does "Act as..." work?

It primes the model's neural network to access patterns associated with that specific role or domain knowledge.
